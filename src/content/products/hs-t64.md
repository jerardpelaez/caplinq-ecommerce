### Directions for Use

Add HumiSeal&reg; Thinner to a compatible coating until the desired viscosity is obtained.

### Handling Precautions

HumiSeal&reg; Thinners are flammable and the following standard safety precautions should be observed:

- Use only in well ventilated areas.

- Protective gloves and goggles should be worn.

- In case of prolonged skin contact, wash with water.

- In case of eye contact, flush with water and contact physician.

- Avoid prolonged breathing of vapors.

- Unused portions of HumiSeal&reg; products must be disposed of in strict compliance with Local, State, and Federal laws governing such matters.

- Consult the Safety Data Sheet (SDS) for more specific product information.
