### Directions for Use

Add HumiSeal Thinner to a compatible coating until the desired viscosity is obtained.

### Handling Precautions

HumiSeal Thinners are flammable and the following standard safety precautions should be observed:

- Use only in well ventilated areas.

- Protective gloves and goggles should be worn.

- In case of prolonged skin contact, wash withwater.

- In case of eye contact, flush with water
