SP-SAC105 is being offered in two options: No-Clean and Water-Soluble. No-clean solder paste is designed for applications where post-soldering cleaning is unnecessary, providing minimal, non-conductive residues. On the other hand, the water-soluble solder paste is crafted with refined tin powder that allows you to clean flux residue left after the reflow process. LINQALLOY SP-SAC105 is produced using refined Sn powder and minimal oxide content, enabling exceptional printing capabilities.
