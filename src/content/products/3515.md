shortDescription: LOCTITE 3515 is a one component epoxy adhesive, designed to allow self-alignment of SMT components during the reflow operation. The adhesive is pre-applied to the board at the corners of the CSP pad site using a standard SMA dispenser, a process that speeds assembly by eliminating post-reflow underfill dispense and cure steps. LOCTITE 3515 cures during the solder reflow phase to form a reliable bond, and contributes to improvements in handheld device reliability. LOCTITE 3515 cornerbond has excellent dispensing characteristics, good dot profile, and good on-board electrical characteristics. Reinforcement of CSPs with interposers or comerless arrays in portable electronic devices. Cure Schedule 30 minutes @ 150 °C 5 minutes @ 180 °C 30 seconds @ 200 °C 15 seconds @ 220 °C
