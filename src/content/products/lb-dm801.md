Together, these products provide a comprehensive solution for encapsulating and protecting electronic components. With its low thermal expansion coefficient (CTE1: 20 ppm/°C, CTE2: 60 ppm/°C), LB-DM801 effectively prevents fill materials from spreading, ensuring precise control of encapsulant placement. Its thixotropic nature (TI: 3.1) and viscosity of 41800 cps at 25°C provide excellent sag resistance and dimensional stability, even on vertical surfaces. Cure Schedule 40 minutes @ 150°C 20 minutes @ 160°C"
