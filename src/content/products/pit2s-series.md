shortDescription: LINQTAPE™ PIT2S-Series is a high-temperature resistant polyimide tape designed for both permanent and temporary bonding in many high temperature applications. These applications can be found in semiconductor, smartcard, electronic, automotive and general manufacturing industries. LINQTAPE™ PIT2S-Series Tape provides an excellent balance of electrical, mechanical, thermal, and chemical properties over a wide range of temperatures. It can be used in applications that may see temperatures up to 260°C (500°F). LINQTAPE™ PIT2S-Series Tape comes in 33 meter (36 yard) rolls of varying widths.
