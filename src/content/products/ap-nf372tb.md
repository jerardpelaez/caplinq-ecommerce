### Process Parameters

Process Parameters
Recommendation

**Flux Deposition**
600 to 1400 ug/in2 of solids (93 to 217 ug/cm2)

**Top Side Board Temperature **
**(Bottom Preheaters Only)**
75 to 120℃

**Top Side Board Temperature**
**(Bottom and Top Preheaters Only)**
95 to 140℃

**Bottom Side Board Temperature**
0 to 32℃ vs top side board temperature

**Recommended Preheat Profile**
Straight tamp to top side board temperature

**Conveyor Speed**
0.5 to 1/2 m/min (1.6 to 3.9 ft/min)

**Solder Contact Time**
3 to 7 seconds

**Solder Bath Temperature**
260 to 270℃ (500 to 518℉) for SnCu or SAC alloy
245 to 260℃ (473 to 500℉) for Sn63Pb37 alloy
