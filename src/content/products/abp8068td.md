What are the MSL and Thermal cycling expectations?


ABP 8068TD has successfully passed MSL3 + 1000 TC (-65/+150C) on PPF QFN device with 3x3mm Ag BSM. As such, we do believe that ABP 8068TD may also pass -40/+125C temp cycling on a larger 4x5mm die size.


For such a larger die size, we do recommend a longer 1hr staging time @ 130°C + minimum 1hr sintering @ 200°C


Cure Schedule


Cure Schedule for Ag, Au and PPF leadframe: 20 minutes ramp to 130°C, hold for 30 minutes, 15 minutes ramp to 200°C, hold for 1 hour in Air or N2


Cure Schedule for Cu leadframe: 20 minutes ramp to 130°C, hold for 30 minutes, 15 minutes ramp to 200°C, hold for 1 hour in N2


Alternate Cure Schedule


Cure Schedule for Ag, Au and PPF leadframe: 20 minutes ramp to 130°C, hold for 30 minutes, 20 minutes ramp to 220°C, hold for 1 hour in Air or N2


Cure Schedule for Cu leadframe: 20 minutes ramp to 130°C, hold for 30 minutes, 20 minutes ramp to 220°C, hold for 1 hour in N2
