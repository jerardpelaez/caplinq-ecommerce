## Key Features of Indium Tin Oxide Films:



- Outstanding Electrical Conductivity: Achieving impressively low resistivity of up to 10-4 Ω∙cm, this film ensures efficient electrical conductivity for various applications.

- High Visible Light Transmittance: With exceptional light transmittance of up to 85%, our ITO Conductive Film guarantees crystal-clear visibility within the visible spectral region.

- UV Absorption: Boasting superior ultraviolet (UV) absorption, with an absorption rate exceeding 85%, this film offers excellent UV protection.

- Infrared Reflectivity: With an infrared (IR) reflectivity exceeding 80%, it efficiently manages heat and radiation, making it suitable for diverse applications.

- Microwave Attenuation: Our ITO Conductive Film excels in microwave attenuation, with an attenuation rate of up to 85%, making it an ideal choice for applications requiring interference reduction.

- Exceptional Durability: The thin layer exhibits high hardness, wear resistance, and chemical corrosion resistance, ensuring long-lasting performance even in challenging environments.

- Ease of Processing: This film offers excellent machining performance and is easy to etch, allowing for effortless customization to meet your specific requirements.
